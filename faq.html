This section provides answers to common questions and issues encountered by users when using the system.  

General Questions

  1. How do I access the system?

Users can access the system by logging in using their registered email and password.

2. What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the “Forgot Password” option on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

3. Why can't I see certain modules?

Access to modules depends on your role and permissions. Contact your administrator if you require access.

Announcements

4. How do I create an announcement?

Go to Announcements ⇢ Click + Add ⇢ Fill in the details ⇢ Submit

5. Can I edit or delete on announcement?

Yes, but only if you have the required permissions.

Calendar

6. Why are some events not visible?

Check your filter (company, location, leave, birthdays). Some data may be hidden based on selected filters.

Meeting Notes

7. How do I create meeting notes?

Go to Meeting Notes ⇢ Click + Add Meeting ⇢ Fill in the details ⇢ Submit

8. Can I assign tasks during a meeting?

Yes, tasks can be added under the Quick Tasks section while creating meeting notes.

Taskboard

9. How do I create a task?

Go to Taskboard ⇢ Click + Create Task ⇢ Fill in the details ⇢ Submit

10. Why is my task not visible?

Ensure the task status is correct (To-do, Done, KIV) and filters are not hiding it.

Approvals

11. How do I approve requests?

Go to your respective space (Manager, Director, Supervisor, HR) ⇢ Open approval module ⇢ Review and approve/reject.

12. Why can't I approve a request?

You may not have the required role or permission.

Account / Finance

13. How do I submit a payment request?

Go to Account Space ⇢ Supplier Payment Request ⇢ Fill in required details ⇢ Submit

14. Where can I track payment status?

Go to Supplier Payment Tracker or Payment History.

Technical Issues

15. What should I do if the system is slow or not loading?

  • Refresh the page
  • Check internet connection
  • Try again later

16. Who should I contact for support?

Go to Support Center in System Settings or contact your system administrator.

Security

17. Is my data secure?

Yes, the system uses secure authentication and database protection (Supabase security features).

18. Can I access the system from any device?

Yes, the system is web-based and accessible from supported browsers.

Other

19. Can I use the system on mobile?

Yes, but the experience may vary depending on screen size.

20. How do I report a bug or issue?

Use the Support Center or Incident Report module to submit issues.
