The Ops Efficiency Space is designed to monitor operational performance and workforce efficiency through attendance tracking and reporting.

It helps ensure that operations teams maintain productivity, accountability, and proper workforce management.  

Overview

With Ops Efficiency Space, users can:

  • Track operations attendance records
  • Monitor workforce availability
  • Identify attendance trends and patterns
  • Support operational planning and decision-making

Reports

This section provides key insights into operations performance through attendance data.

Operations Attendance Record

  • Track attendance for operations teams
  • Monitor daily workforce presence
  • Identify absenteeism or irregular attendance
  • Support workforce planning and reporting

Key Functions

The Operations Attendance Record allows users to:

  • Review attendance data in real-time
  • Identify attendance issues early
  • Improve workforce efficiency
  • Ensure accountability across operations teams

Tips

  • Regularly review attendance records to detect patterns
  • Address absenteeism early to maintain productivity
  • Use data insights to optimise workforce planning
  • Ensure accurate attendance tracking for reporting purposes
