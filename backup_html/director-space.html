The Director Space is designed for directors to review and approve submissions from managers across all business units.

It provides a high-level overview of key activities, ensuring decisions are aligned with organisational goals.  

Overview

With Director Space, users can:

  • Review and approve submissions from managers
  • Monitor cross-department activities
  • Access summarised reports and records
  • Oversee operational and strategic workflows

Claim Management

General Approvals

  • Review and approve general claims submitted by managers
  • Ensure claims are valid and aligned with company policies

Leave Management

Leave Approvals

Review and approve employee leave requests escalated from managers.

Leave History 

Track and review team leave records across departments.

Feedback Tickets

Incident Report Review

  • Review reported incidents requiring director-level attention
  • Take action on operational or critical issues

Improvement Suggestions

  • Review and monitor improvement ideas submitted by teams
  • Evaluate suggestions that require higher-level visibility

Key Responsibilities

Directors are responsible for:

  • Making final approval decisions for critical requests
  • Ensuring compliance with company policies
  • Monitoring overall team and departmental performance
  • Supporting continuous improvement initiatives

Tips

  • Prioritise approvals that impact multiple departments
  • Review reports regularly to maintain oversight
  • Ensure all decisions align with organisational objectives
  • Monitor feedback and incidents for continuous improvement
