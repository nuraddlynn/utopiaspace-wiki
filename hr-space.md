## Common Workflow

<div class="utopia-hr-flow">
  <div>
    <strong>1</strong>
    <span>Open HR Space from the sidebar.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>2</strong>
    <span>Select the HR area you need, such as employee records, leave, attendance, benefits, or reports.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>3</strong>
