The Account Space is designed for finance and accounting teams to manage financial transactions, verify claims, and process payments.

It includes multiple sub-modules to handle different accounting workflows efficiently.  

Overview

With Account Space, users can:

  • Process financial applications and payments
  • Verify and approve claims
  • Track financial records and history
  • Manage supplier and backcharge data
  • Access reports and analytics

Sidebar Structure

The Account Space consists of the following sub-modules:

  • Account Menu
  • AP Workspace (Account Payable)

Account Menu

The Account Menu focuses on verification, approvals, and financial tracking.

Applications

  • Supplier Payment Request - Submit supplier payment requests initiated by the finance team
  • Backcharge Application - Submit intracompany backcharge requests

Verify & Approve

  • General Claims - Review and verify claims submitted from Manager Space
  • Benefit Claims - Verify employee benefit claims submitted from HR Space
  • Advance Salary Verification - Verify advance salary requests approved by HR
  • SGS Commission - Review and verify SGS commission applications
  • Backcharges - Verify and process intracompany backcharge requests
  • Supplier Payments - Verify and process supplier payment submissions

Reports & History

  • Supplier Payment Tracker - Monitor all supplier payment requests
  • Supplier Payment History - View past supplier payment records (read-only)
  • Backcharge History - View intercompany backcharge records
  • Petty Cash Overview - View petty cash submissions and approvals

Setup & Reference

  • Supplier List - Manage supplier reference data
  • Item Code List - Maintain item codes used for payment verification

Incident Report Management

  • Incident Report Review - Verify finance-related incidents and documentation

AP Workspace (Account Payable)

The AP Workspace handles payment processing and financial execution

Bank Processing

  • Claims Payments - Reconcile and submit approved claims to the bank
  • Backcharge Payments - Process approved backcharge payments
  • Supplier Payments - Process approved supplier payments for bank submission

Reports & Analytics

  • AP Analytics - View KPIs and financial trends
  • Payment Summary - Access a centralised view of all payment records

Advance Salary (AP)

  • Advance Salary (AP) - Submit approved advance salary requests for bank processing

Tips

  • Always verify claims before processing payments
  • Ensure all approvals are completed before submission to bank
  • Use analytics to monitor financial performance
  • Maintain accurate supplier and item reference data
