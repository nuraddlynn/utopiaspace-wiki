# Director Space

<span class="utopia-director-scope"></span>

Use Director Space to review director-level approvals, monitor cross-department activity, and make decisions aligned with organisational goals.

<div class="utopia-director-actions">
  <a href="/manager-space">Manager Space</a>
  <a href="/supervisor-space">Supervisor Space</a>
  <a href="/hr-space">HR Space</a>
</div>

## Overview

<div class="utopia-director-grid">
  <section>
    <h3>Approve Escalations</h3>
    <p>Review submissions escalated from managers and make final approval decisions where required.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Monitor Operations</h3>
    <p>Track cross-department activity, reports, records, and operational workflow status.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Guide Decisions</h3>
    <p>Ensure approvals and follow-up actions stay aligned with policies and business objectives.</p>
  </section>
</div>

## Claim Management

<div class="utopia-director-table">
  <div class="utopia-director-head">Area</div>
  <div class="utopia-director-head">Director responsibility</div>

  <strong>General Approvals</strong>
  <p>Review and approve general claims submitted or escalated by managers.</p>

  <strong>Policy Alignment</strong>
  <p>Check whether claims are valid, supported, and aligned with company policies.</p>
</div>

## Leave Management

<div class="utopia-director-table">
  <div class="utopia-director-head">Area</div>
  <div class="utopia-director-head">Use</div>

  <strong>Leave Approvals</strong>
  <p>Review and approve employee leave requests escalated from managers.</p>

  <strong>Leave History</strong>
  <p>Track and review team leave records across departments.</p>
</div>

## Feedback Tickets

<div class="utopia-director-table">
  <div class="utopia-director-head">Ticket Area</div>
  <div class="utopia-director-head">What directors review</div>

  <strong>Incident Report Review</strong>
  <p>Review reported incidents that require director-level attention or action.</p>

  <strong>Improvement Suggestions</strong>
  <p>Review improvement ideas submitted by teams and evaluate suggestions requiring higher-level visibility.</p>
</div>

## Key Responsibilities

<div class="utopia-director-table">
  <div class="utopia-director-head">Responsibility</div>
  <div class="utopia-director-head">Focus</div>

  <strong>Final Decisions</strong>
  <p>Make approval decisions for critical or escalated requests.</p>

  <strong>Compliance</strong>
  <p>Ensure decisions follow company policy and internal governance expectations.</p>

  <strong>Performance Oversight</strong>
  <p>Monitor overall team and departmental performance from a high-level view.</p>

  <strong>Continuous Improvement</strong>
  <p>Support improvement initiatives by reviewing feedback, incidents, and recurring operational issues.</p>
</div>

## Common Workflow

<div class="utopia-director-flow">
  <div>
    <strong>1</strong>
    <span>Open Director Space from the sidebar.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>2</strong>
    <span>Review escalated approvals, reports, leave records, or feedback tickets.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>3</strong>
    <span>Check supporting details, policy alignment, and cross-department impact.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>4</strong>
    <span>Approve, reject, or follow up based on organisational priorities.</span>
  </div>
</div>

## Tips

<div class="utopia-director-grid">
  <section>
    <h3>Prioritise Impact</h3>
    <p>Start with approvals or incidents that affect multiple departments.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Review Reports</h3>
    <p>Use reports regularly to maintain visibility across teams and business units.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Watch Feedback</h3>
    <p>Monitor incidents and suggestions for patterns that may need wider improvement work.</p>
  </section>
</div>
