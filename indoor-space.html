The Indoor Space is designed to support indoor sales operations by providing access to essential tools, documents, and quotation tracking.

It helps sales teams manage calculations, maintain records, and ensure consistency in indoor sales processes.  

Overview

With Indoor Space, users can:

  • Access sales guideline documents
  • Perform ceiling and cost calculations
  • Track quotation history
  • Maintain structured indoor sales workflows

Document Management

Document Management 

  • Store and access indoor sales guideline documents
  • Ensure standardisation across sales processes
  • Provide easy reference for team members

Sales Tools

Ceiling Calculator 

  • Calculate ceiling measurements and costing
  • Assist in generating accurate quotations
  • Improve efficiency and reduce manual errors

Quotation History

  • Track all past quotations
  • Review pricing and customer records
  • Support decision-making for future sales

Key Functions

The Indoor Space allows users to:

  • Standardise sales documentation
  • Improve quotation accuracy
  • Track historical sales data
  • Enhance productivity in indoor operations

Tips

  • Always refer to guideline documents before preparing quotations
  • Double-check calculations using the calculator tool
  • Use quotation history to improve pricing strategies
  • Keep records updated for better tracking and reporting
