<p>The Announcements feature allows users to create, publish, and manage important updates within the system.</p>
<p>It is used to communicate information to selected users, departments, or the entire organisation. &nbsp;</p>
<h1>Overview</h1>
<p>With this feature, users can:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Create new announcements</li>
  <li>Add titles, summaries, and detailed content</li>
  <li>Select target companies and departments</li>
  <li>Upload banner images</li>
  <li>Publish immediately or schedule announcements</li>
</ul>
<h1>Creating an Announcement</h1>
<p>To create a new announcement:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Click on <i>+ Add</i> button on the top right corner</li>
  <li>Fill in the required fields</li>
</ol>
<h2>Title</h2>
<p>Enter a clear and concise title for the announcement.</p>
<h2>Summary</h2>
<p>Provide a short description that will appear on announcement cards.</p>
<h2>Type</h2>
<p>Select the announcement type (e.g., Announcement, System Update, Event/Social, Official Memo)</p>
<h2>Company</h2>
<p>Choose the company or companies the announcement applies to.</p>
<h2>Department</h2>
<p>Select relevant departments (if applicable)</p>
<h1>Publish Settings</h1>
<h2>Publish Now</h2>
<p>The announcement will be published immediately.</p>
<h2>Schedule</h2>
<p>Set a specific date and time for the announcement to be published.</p>
<h1>Banner Image</h1>
<ul>
  <li>Upload an image to make the announcement more visually engaging</li>
  <li>Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP</li>
  <li>Recommended size: 1200 x 600 (2:1 ratio)</li>
</ul>
<h1>Content</h1>
<ul>
  <li>Enter the full announcement details in the content editor</li>
  <li>You may format text and attach supporting files if needed</li>
</ul>
<h1>Preview</h1>
<p>Use the <i>Live Preview</i> section to view how the announcement will appear before publishing.</p>
<h1>Saving and Submitting</h1>
<ul>
  <li>Click <i>Save as Draft</i> to save without publishing</li>
  <li>Click <i>Submit</i> to publish the announcement</li>
</ul>
<h1>Tips</h1>
<ul>
  <li>Use clear and professional language in announcements</li>
  <li>Keep the summary short and informative</li>
  <li>Ensure the correct company and department are selected</li>
  <li>Preview before publishing to avoid errors</li>
</ul>
