# Accessing the System

1.  Open your web browser (e.g., Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge)
2.  Enter the system URL
    ([space.utopiagroup.com.my](https://space.utopiagroup.com.my)) in
    the address bar
3.  Press *Enter* to open the login page

# Login Steps

1.  Click on the *Sign in with Google* button
2.  Select your Google account
3.  Enter your Google credentials if prompted
4.  Grant the required permissions (if any)

You will be automatically redirected to the system dashboard upon
successful login.

# First-Time Login

If this is your first time accessing the system:

- Ensure you are using your registered Google account
- Your access level will be assigned based on your role (e.g., Manager,
  HR, Accounts)
- Contact the administrator if you are unable to access certain features

# Login Issues

If you encounter issues during login:

- Make sure you are using the correct Google account
- Ensure your Google account has been granted access to the system
- Try logging out and signing in again
- Clear your browser cache if the issue persists
- Contact the system administrator for assistance

# Security Tips

- Always use your official company Google account
- Do not share access to your account
- Log out from shared or public devices after use
