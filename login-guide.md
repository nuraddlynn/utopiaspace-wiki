## Accessing the System

<div class="utopia-login-flow">
  <div>
    <strong>1</strong>
    <span>Open your web browser, such as Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>2</strong>
    <span>Go to <a href="https://space.utopiagroup.com.my">space.utopiagroup.com.my</a>.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>3</strong>
    <span>Press Enter to open the UtopiaSpace login page.</span>
  </div>
</div>

## Login Steps

<div class="utopia-login-flow">
  <div>
    <strong>1</strong>
    <span>Click <b>Sign in with Google</b>.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>2</strong>
    <span>Select your registered company Google account.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>3</strong>
    <span>Enter your Google credentials if prompted.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>4</strong>
    <span>Grant required permissions if Google asks for confirmation.</span>
  </div>
</div>

After successful login, you will be redirected to the system dashboard.

## First-Time Login

<div class="utopia-login-grid">
  <section>
    <h3>Use the Correct Account</h3>
    <p>Make sure you are signing in with your registered company Google account.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Check Your Role</h3>
    <p>Your access level is assigned based on your role, such as Manager, HR, Accounts, or another department.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Confirm Access</h3>
    <p>If some features are missing, contact the administrator to confirm your permissions.</p>

## Login Issues

<div class="utopia-login-table">
  <div class="utopia-login-head">Issue</div>
  <div class="utopia-login-head">What to check</div>

  <strong>Wrong account</strong>
  <p>Sign out and choose the Google account registered for UtopiaSpace.</p>

  <strong>No access</strong>
  <p>Ask the administrator to confirm your account has been granted access.</p>

  <strong>Stuck after login</strong>
  <p>Try logging out, signing in again, or opening the system in a fresh browser tab.</p>

  <strong>Page still fails</strong>
  <p>Clear your browser cache, then retry. If it continues, contact the system administrator.</p>
</div>

## Security Tips

<div class="utopia-login-grid">
  <section>
    <h3>Use Company Login</h3>
    <p>Always use your official company Google account.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Keep Access Private</h3>
    <p>Do not share your account access or Google credentials with anyone.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Log Out on Shared Devices</h3>
    <p>Always sign out after using UtopiaSpace on shared or public devices.</p>
  </section>
