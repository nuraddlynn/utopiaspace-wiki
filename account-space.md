# Account Space

<span class="utopia-account-scope"></span>

Use Account Space to manage finance workflows, verify claims, process payments, monitor supplier records, and track financial activity.

<div class="utopia-account-actions">

  <a href="/manager-space">Manager Space</a>

## Overview

<div class="utopia-account-grid">
  <section>
    <h3>Verify Finance Requests</h3>
    <p>Review claims, supplier payments, backcharges, commissions, and salary advance submissions before payment processing.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Process Payments</h3>
    <p>Prepare approved claims, supplier payments, backcharges, and advance salary requests for bank processing.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Track Financial Records</h3>
    <p>Monitor payment history, supplier records, petty cash, item codes, reports, and AP analytics.</p>
  </section>
</div>

## Common Workflow

<div class="utopia-account-flow">
  <div>
    <strong>1</strong>
    <span>Open Account Space from the sidebar.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>2</strong>
    <span>Select the relevant finance area, such as claims, supplier payment, backcharge, or AP Workspace.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>3</strong>
    <span>Review the request details, supporting records, approvals, and reference data.</span>
  </div>
  <div>
    <strong>4</strong>
    <span>Verify, process, submit, or track the request according to the finance workflow.</span>
  </div>
</div>

## Key Responsibilities

<div class="utopia-account-table">
  <div class="utopia-account-head">Responsibility</div>
  <div class="utopia-account-head">Focus</div>

  <strong>Verification</strong>
  <p>Check claims, supplier payments, backcharges, commissions, and salary advance requests before processing.</p>

  <strong>Payment Processing</strong>
  <p>Prepare approved finance requests for bank processing through AP Workspace.</p>

  <strong>Record Accuracy</strong>
  <p>Maintain reliable supplier lists, item codes, histories, trackers, and payment summaries.</p>

  <strong>Financial Monitoring</strong>
  <p>Use reports and analytics to monitor finance activity and follow up on pending items.</p>
</div>

## Tips

<div class="utopia-account-grid">
  <section>
    <h3>Verify First</h3>
    <p>Always complete verification before moving a request into payment processing.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Check References</h3>
    <p>Keep supplier and item code records accurate so payment requests remain clean.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h3>Use Trackers</h3>
    <p>Use payment trackers, histories, and analytics to monitor pending and completed finance work.</p>
  </section>
</div>
